Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 23, 2025

Welcome to ServiceLink. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you use ServiceLink:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, profile picture, and location
  • Service Provider Information: Professional details, service categories, rates, availability, and certifications
  • Service Requests: Details about services you request or provide
  • Communications: Messages exchanged through our chat system
  • Payment Information: Transaction details (processed securely through third-party payment processors)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app usage data
  • Location Data: Your location for finding nearby service providers and enabling location-based features

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve ServiceLink services
  • To connect service seekers with service providers
  • To process service requests and transactions
  • To facilitate communication between users
  • To send notifications about service requests, messages, and updates
  • To verify user identities and maintain platform safety
  • To provide customer support
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following situations:

  • With Other Users: Your profile information is visible to other users to facilitate service connections
  • With Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate the app (Firebase, Google Cloud, etc.)
  • For Legal Reasons: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, sale, or acquisition
Note: We will never sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication through Firebase
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication requirements

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information through the app
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Opt-out: Disable notifications and location services in app settings
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format

6. Location Services

ServiceLink uses your location to:

  • Show nearby service providers
  • Calculate distances and service areas
  • Improve search results

You can disable location services in your device settings, but this may limit certain features of the app.

7. Third-Party Services

ServiceLink integrates with third-party services including:

  • Firebase: Authentication, database, and storage
  • Google Sign-In: Authentication services
  • Google Maps: Location and mapping services

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

8. Children's Privacy

ServiceLink is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

10. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. When you delete your account, we will remove or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.
